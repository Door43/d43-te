====== shrine ======

===== Definition: =====

A shrine is a place which is dedicated to the worship of a deity.

  * A shrine would normally contain some type of altar as a place to offer a sacrifice to the deity.
  * In the Bible the use of the term shrine refers to a place of pagan deity worship, as in the Apostle Paul describing the shrine of the Greek Goddess Diana.

(See also: other)
