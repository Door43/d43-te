====== freewill offering ======

===== Definition: =====

A voluntary offering given completely at the desire of the donor. These were above and beyond the required offerings of the Jewish law.

  * The book of Ezra records the Israelites bringing many freewill offerings to help rebuild their temple.
  * The freewill offering was a cause of rejoicing for Israel since it reflected times of plenty and successful crops, etc.

(See also: other)
