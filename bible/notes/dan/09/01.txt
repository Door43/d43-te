===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who had been made king over the realm of the Babylonians** - AT: "who became king over the realm of the Babylonians" or "who conquered the Babylonians" (UDB). (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **over the realm** - "over the country" or "over the kingdom"
  * **there would be seventy years until Jerusalem's abandonment would end** - "from the time Jerusalem was destroyed, it would be abandoned for 70 years" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
