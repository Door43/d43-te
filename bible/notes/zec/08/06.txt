===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If something seems impossible in the eyes of**  - "If something does not appear to be possible to"
  * **the remnant of this people**  - AT: "the people of Judah who survive"
  * **should it also seem impossible in my eyes?** - AT: "it will surely be possible for me!" (See: [[te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **I am about to rescue my people** - "I am about to rescue my people of Judah who went into exile" (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
