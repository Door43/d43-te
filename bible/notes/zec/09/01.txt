===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is a declaration of Yahweh's word concerning** - AT: "This is Yahweh's message about"
  * **the land of Hadrach and Damascus** - AT: "the people of the land of Hadrach and the city Damascus (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for Yahweh's eye is on all mankind** - AT: "for Yahweh is watching all mankind"
  * **Hamath** - AT: "the people of the land of Hamath" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Tyre and Sidon** - AT: "the people of Tyre and Sidon" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
