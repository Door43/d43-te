===== Translation Notes: =====

The people of God are speaking in 57:1, and God speaks in 57:2-3. See: [[:te:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **Al Tashheth**  - See how you translated this in [[:te:bible:notes:psa:057:001|57:1]].
  * **A psalm of Asaph, a song**  - AT: “This is a psalm that Asaph wrote, a song.”
  * **all the inhabitants** - "all the people who live on it"
  * **make steady the earth's pillars**  - "keep the earth from being destroyed" or AT: "keep my people safe" (See: [[te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Selah**  - See how you translated this in [[:te:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:2]].
