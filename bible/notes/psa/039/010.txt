===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Stop wounding me**  - AT: "cease striking me" or, O' my God, cease your blows upon me"
    * **I am overwhelmed**  - AT: "I am defeated completely"
    * **When you discipline people for sin**  - AT: "When you rebuke someone and punish him for the sin" (UDB)
    * **like a moth** - AT: "like moths eat away at clothing" (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **people are nothing but vapor** - AT: "Our lives disappear like a puff of air" (UDB) or, "Our lives are completely fragile" (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
