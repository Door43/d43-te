===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[te:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] 

  * **chief musician** - This could be the leader of the music for worship.
  * **set to the Sheminith** - See how you translated this in [[:te:bible:notes:psa:006:001|6:1]].
  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David." or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **Help, Yahweh** - AT: "Yahweh, come to my aid,"
  * **faithful have vanished** - "faithful people have all vanished."
