===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Create in me a clean heart, God,**  - The author is asking for God to remove sinful desire from him. AT: "O God, cause my inner being to be pure," (UDB) (See: Idiom)
  * **and renew a right spirit within me.**  This is very similar to the first line, asking God to help the author to live a righteous life. (See: Idiom and Doublet)
  * **Do not drive me**  - "Do not send me"
  * **and do not take your holy Spirit from me.**  - (See: Doublet)
