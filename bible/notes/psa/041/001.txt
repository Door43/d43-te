===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[:te:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

    * **For the chief musician**  - AT: "Send this to the priest in charge of the king's music."
    * **A psalm of David**  - AT: "A psalm that David wrote." 
    * **Blessed is he who is concerned for the weak**  - AT:"Yahweh blesses those people who help to provide for the poor" (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **Yahweh will rescue those people when they have troubles**  - The psalmist shows the importance of providing for the poor by listing several benefits God gives those who do this.
