===== Translation Notes: =====
See: talink [[:te:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

    * **For the chief musician. A psalm of David.**  - (This psalm is sent to the leader of the musicians of Israel. It is written by David.)
    * ****  - explanation
    * **I waited patiently for Yahweh**  - This implies that the writer has been praying to Yahweh, and was waiting for an answer. (See: Implicit Information)
    * **he listened to me and heard my cry**  - This implies that the writer's prayers were answered. (See Implicit Information) (See: Personification)
    * **out of a horrible pit, out of the miry clay**  - AT: "out of my trouble" (See Doublet and Metaphor)
    * **set my feet on a rock and established my going.**  - AT: "stabilized my situation and enabled me to start again on the right path." (See Metaphor)
