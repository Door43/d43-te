===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I have not concealed...I have declared...**  - (See: Parallelism)
    * **I have not concealed your righteousness in my heart;**  - AT: "I have not kept silent about what you have revealed to me about your holiness, Yahweh."
    * **I have not concealed your covenant faithfulness or your trustworthiness from the great assembly.**  - AT: "I have told all the people about how faithfully you keep your promises to us, and how we can rely upon them."
    * **Do not keep back your acts of mercy from me...**  - AT: "Bless me with your mercy..." 
    * **let your covenant faithfulness and your trustworthiness always preserve me.**  - AT: "let your faithful word, which I can trust, keep me safe."
