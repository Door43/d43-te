(See: Translating Poetry and Parallelism)

  * **together like a heap **- AT: "into one huge mass" (UDB)
  * **it was done **  - The writer is refering back to the gathering of waters and ahead to creation of all that exists. (See: Translate Pronouns)
  * **he commanded, and it stood in place **- AT: "Everything started to exist when he commanded it to be" (UDB)
