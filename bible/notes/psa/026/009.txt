===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nor my life with bloody men** - AT: "and do not sweep away my life with bloody men"  (See: [[te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **bloody men** - AT: "men who shed blood" or "people who kill others"
  * **in whose hands there is a plot** - "people who plan to do wicked things"
  * **a plot** - "a wicked plan"
