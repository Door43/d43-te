===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the council of the holy ones** - AT: "the gathering of heavenly beings"
  * **who is strong like you, Yahweh?** The writer asks the question to emphasize there is no one like Yahweh. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Your truthfulness surrounds you.**  - AT: "Your faithfulness surrounds you." (See: [[te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
