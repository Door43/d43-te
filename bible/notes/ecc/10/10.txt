===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **wisdom provides an advantage for success.**  - AT: wisdom provides a good condition for success.
    * **If a snake bites before it is charmed, then there is no advantage for the charmer.**  - AT: "If a snake bites before it is calmed, then there is no good result for the person calming the snake."
