===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **takes a wife and marries her** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **if she finds no favor in his eyes**  - "if he decides that he does not approve of her" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **he has found some unsuitable thing in her**  - Possible meanings are 1) "he has decided that she is an unacceptable wife for some reason or another", or 2) "he has determined that she has committed some moral or sexual sin."
    * **he must write her a bill of divorcement**  - Usually, divorce was the decision of the husband alone. This "bill" was an official paper that stated that he had divorced his wife.
    * **she may go and be another man's wife**  - If a woman was formally divorced, she was legally free to marry another man.
