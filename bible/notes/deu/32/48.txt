===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Go up into his range or the Abarim mountains - **  Yahweh tells Moses to go to a higher place so that he can show Moses the Promised Land/land of Canaan, from up there.
  * **Abarim mountains**  - (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mount Nebo**  - One of the mountains of Abarim. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **land of Moab**- It used to belong to the Moabites but now it was possessed by Israel.
