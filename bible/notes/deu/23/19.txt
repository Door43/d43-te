\v 20 To a foreigner you may lend on interest; but to your fellow Israelite you must not lend on interest, so that Yahweh your God may bless you in all that you put your hand to, in the land which you are going in to possess.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lend on interest - ** “charge him extra money for borrowing” or “charge him extra money for the loan”
  * **or the interest of anything that is lent on interest **- or anything else (UDB)
  * **all that you put your hand to**  - AT: " everything you have worked for” or “all that you have undertaken” (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
