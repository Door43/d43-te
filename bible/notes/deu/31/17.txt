===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my anger will be kindled against them**  - Like a destructive fire, God will become very angry with Israel. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])  (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my**  - Yahweh continues to speak.
  * **I will hide my face from them**  - AT: "I will not be present to help them" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])  (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they will be devoured**  - God in his anger will destroy them as with a fierce fire. AT: "destruction will come upon them" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])  (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Many disasters and troubles will find them**  - Like people, disasters and troubles look for the Israelites in order to harm them. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **‘Have these disasters not found me because our God is not in my midst?’**  - This emphasizes that the Israelites will realize that their troubles are due to God's absence. AT: 'These disasters have happened to me because God is absent." (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] )  (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
