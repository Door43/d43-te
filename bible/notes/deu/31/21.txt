===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When many evils and troubles find this people**  - This speaks of evil and trouble as if they were able to search for people to punish. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **this song will testify before it as a witness**  - This speaks of the song as if it were a human witness testifying to Israel. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **it will not be forgotten out of the mouths of their descendants**  - AT: "their descendents will remember this song" (UDB) (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
