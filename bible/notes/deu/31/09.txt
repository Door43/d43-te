===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Moses wrote this Law and gave it out to the priests**  - AT: "Moses wrote this Law on a scroll and gave the scroll to the priests."
  * **gave it out to the priests, the sons of Levi** - gave it out to the Levitical priests.
  * **Festival of Shelters - **  AT: “Feast of Tabernacles” or “Festival of Booths” or “Feast of Ingathering”. The idea came from the practice of the farmers living in temporary booths, or huts, out in the fields to guard the crop as it ripened. See how you translated in Exodus 23:16.
  * **At the end of every seven years**  - Every seventh year was a year of rest to the land and of remission of debts.
  * **cancellation of debts**  - A debt is what one person owes another.
  * **when all Israel has come to appear before Yahweh your God**  - AT: "when all the people of Israel have come to the central sanctuary of Yahweh your God"
  * ** in the place that he will choose for his sanctuary ** - From the territory divided between the tribes of Israel, Yahweh would chose one location for worship. That later came to be Jerusalem, where the temple was built, inside the territory of the tribe of Judah.
  * ** in their hearing ** - so that they can hear it
