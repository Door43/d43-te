===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Blessed be he who enlarges Gad.**  - This could be an expression of thanks to God for giving Gad a large territory or for bringing him out of trouble, since Gad was surrounded by powerful enemies.
    * **He will live there like a lioness**  - Gad is compared to a lioness; he is secure because of his boldness. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
