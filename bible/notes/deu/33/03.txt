===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the people**  - the people of the twelve tribes of Israel
    * **all his holy people are in your hand**  - The pronouns "his" and "your" refer to Yahweh. The original Hebrew is not always consistent in the use of pronouns. "In your hand" refers to Yahweh's power and protection. (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) AT: "All your people are in your power" or "Yahweh protects those who belong to him." 
    * **received your words**  - "listened to your instructions" (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **inheritance**  - An inheritance is a valuable possession passed on from one generation to the next. AT: "possession" or "precious possession"
