===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The priest must make atonement **- This means that the priest must show that the people of Israel are sorry for their sin.
  * **They will be forgiven **  - AT: "Yahweh will forgive" (See: passive)
  * **all the community of the people of Israel will be forgiven **  - AT: "Yahweh will forgive all the community of the people of Israel" (See: passive)
