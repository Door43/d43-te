===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Balaam got up in the morning, saddled his donkey**  - A saddle is an object that is put on the back of the donkey, so that it could be ridden.
  * **God’s anger was kindled **- This means that God was angry, because he knew Balaam was hoping to get money.  (See: 2 Peter 2:15)
  * **The angel of Yahweh placed himself in the road as someone hostile to Balaam**  - AT: "The angel stood in the road to block Balaam's path." (See: UDB)
