===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do I now have any power to say anything**  - Balaam is rebuking Balak, since Balaam cannot say anything he wants, but will only say what God wants.  AT: "I do not have the power to say anything." (See: Rhetorical Question).
  * **puts into my mouth **-This means the words God will have me say. (See: Idiom)
