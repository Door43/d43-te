===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Should I not be careful to say only what Yahweh puts in my mouth **- Balaam is rebuking Balak, since it would not be good to give a message other than what Yahweh gave.  AT:  I should only give the message that Yahweh gives to me.  (See: Rhetorical Question)
