===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who can count the dust of Jacob**  -****  This means that the people of Israel are so numerous, trying to count them would be like trying to count particles of dust. (See Metaphor)
  * **and let my life's end be like his**  - "His" refers to the nation of Israel.
