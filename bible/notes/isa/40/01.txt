===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Comfort, comfort** - The word "comfort" is repeated for emphasis. 
  * **says your God** - Here "your" is plural and refers to God's people. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **to her that her warfare...her iniquity...she has** - Here "her" and "she" refers to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **her warfare** - "their forced labor" or "their struggles"
  * **from Yahweh's hand** - "from Yahweh." Yahweh is speaking about himself. AT: "from me" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
