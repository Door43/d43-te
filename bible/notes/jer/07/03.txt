===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh of hosts, God of Israel**  - These phrases mean God of heaven and earth
  * **Make your ways and practices good**  - Yahweh is telling Israel to make their ways and practices good (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **ways and practices**  - Yahweh is telling Israel to live the way he taught them to
  * **Do not entrust yourself to deceitful words and say**
 - AT: "People of Jerusalem do not place your trust in those who speak deceitful words, by saying, "Temple of Yahweh! Temple of Yahweh! Temple of Yahweh!" (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]])
  * **Temple of Yahweh! Temple of Yahweh! Temple of Yahweh!**  - This triplet means because the temple is here, Yahweh is also here. (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
