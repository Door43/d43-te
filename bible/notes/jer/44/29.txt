===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This will be the sign for you'—this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - AT: "Yahweh declares, 'This will be proof'"
  * **my words will certainly attack you with disaster**  - AT: "what I said will happen and you will have disaster"
  * **I am about to give over Pharaoh Hophra, king of Egypt, into the hand of his enemies** - AT: "I will allow Pharaoh Hophra's enemies to capture him" or "I will let the enemies of Pharaoh Hophra, king of Egypt, conquer him." (See: Metonymy)
  * **Hophra** - This is the name of a man. (See: Translate Names)
  * **and the ones seeking his life**  - AT: "and the the people who want to kill him" (See: Euphemism)
  * **who sought his life**  - AT: "who wanted to kill him"
