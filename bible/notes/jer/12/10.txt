===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **shepherds**  - in this case shepherds does mean not a provider it means leader or ruler (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **my vineyard**  - the Promised Land, Yahweh's beautiful, fertile land (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **stomped all over** - crushed under foot, destroyed
    * **my portion of land**  - another referenced to the Promised Land
    * **her**  - Promised Land (See:[[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **she**  - Promised Land
    * **for there is no one**  - "and no one"
    * **takes it to heart** - cares (See:[[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
