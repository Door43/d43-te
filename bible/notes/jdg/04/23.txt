===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jabin**  - See how you translated this in [[:te:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **before the people of Israel** - If something is before you it is in front of you where you can see.  To be able to see something is associated with being able to understand it. AT: "in a way so the people of Israel could understand God had enabled the victory" (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The might of the people** - The word "might" refers to the military power of Israel. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **stronger and stronger** - The word "stronger" is repeated to express the people of Israel's strength developed over time. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
