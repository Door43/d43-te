===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **why did he wander about on ships?** - This question is asked to express that the people of the tribe of Dan believed other things were more important.  AT: "The people of the tribe of Dan thought it was more important to explore the sea in ships."  (See: [[te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
  * **Naphtali, also** - AT: "Naphtali, also, would risk their lives to the point of death" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
