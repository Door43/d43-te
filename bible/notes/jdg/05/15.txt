===== Translation Notes: =====

   * **And my princes**  - "And Deborah's princes"
   * **Deborah** - See how you translated her name in [[:te:bible:notes:jdg:04:04|4:4]].
   * **Barak**  - See how you translated his name in [[:te:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
   * **rushing after** - "following"
   * **searchings of heart** - The people of Reuben are described as searching the part of the body associated with emotional decisions. AT: "decisions to be made" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])  The people of Reuben needed to decide if they were willing to go to war with Deborah and Barak. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
