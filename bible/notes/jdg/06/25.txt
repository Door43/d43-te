===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **seven years old** - "7 years old" (See: [[te:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **that is beside it**  - "it" refers to the altar of Baal.
  * **this place of refuge**  - "this hill" See UDB
  * **constructed in the correct way**  - "constructed of stone"  Gideon was aware that there were additional historical instructions on how to build an appropriate altar to Yahweh.
