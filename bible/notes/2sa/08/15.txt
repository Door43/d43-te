===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **David reigned over all Israel**  - Here "Israel" implies the whole nation of Israel (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **righteousness to all his people**  - Here "all his people" also refers to the whole nation of Israel over which David was ruling. (See also: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **Zeruiah…Ahilud … Ahitub…Ahimelech…Seraiah…Jehoiada…Kerethites…Pelethites**  ( See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
