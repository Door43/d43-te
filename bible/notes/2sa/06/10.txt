===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** David was not willing to take the ark of Yahweh with him into the City of David. Instead, he put it aside ** - this is why David feared Yahweh and how he displayed that fear
  * **ark of Yahweh **  - This has the same meaning as the ark of God and the ark of the covenant
  * **Obed Edom the Gittite**  - (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **three months**  - a passage of time (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
