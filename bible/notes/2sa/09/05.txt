===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See, I am your servant!**  - Here the "your" refers only to David since Mephibosheth is addressing David. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of You]])
  * **Mephibosheth**  - Son of Jonathan, son of King Saul, favored by King David (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
