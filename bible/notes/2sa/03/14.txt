===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **one hundred**  - represents the number of men killed by David for his wife (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **foreskin**  - the fold of skin that covers the male reproductive organ
  * **Paltiel, Laish ** - names of people (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/translate/translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Bahurim **- A village between the fords of the Jordan River and Jerusalem.
