===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and have cut off all your enemies**  - The "cut off" means "killed" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **like the name of the great ones who are on the earth**  - The "great ones" means famous persons (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **you**  and **your**  - All of the occurrences of these words refer only to David since Yahweh/God is directly addressing David. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of You]])
