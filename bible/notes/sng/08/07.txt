===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Surging waters cannot quench love nor can floods sweep it away. **- The woman is comparing their love to fire. She is also saying that water represents outside events. Nothing can separate the lovers. (See: Hyperbole) (See: Metaphor)
  * **If a man gave all his possessions...the offer would utterly be despised. **- AT: "The man who thinks he can buy love by offering wealth to a woman will be disappointed because love can only be freely given."
