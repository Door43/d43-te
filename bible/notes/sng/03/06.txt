===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a column of smoke **- "column" – smoke rises from its source into the air. A column is an object that appears to rise (usually) from the ground into the air to give support to another piece of a building. Smoke looks like a column as it rises. (See: metaphor)
  * **perfumed with myrrh and frankincense **- "perfume" – usually a liquid that smells very nice, often made from flowers and put on different areas of the body
  * **with all powders **- "powders" – a fine dust made by crushing something solid, for example, crushing flower petals to make perfume.
  * **sold by merchants**  - "merchant" is a person who buys and sells or trades items for money or other items.
  * **it is the portable litter **- "portable" means easily moved from one place to another. "Litter" refers to a chair or bed with curtains around it that can be moved by men or animals.
  * **sixty warriors**  - a "warrior" is a brave man who fights for and defends his country, village and/or leader.
  * **sixty warriors surround it **- "surround" – are all around it
  * **sixty of Israel's soldiers **- "soldier" – see above, "warrior"
