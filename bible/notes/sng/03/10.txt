===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **its posts were made of gold**  - "post" is a long strong piece of wood
  * **Its interior was**  - "interior" is the inside
  * **decorated with love**  - "decorate" is to make pretty.  "Decorated with love" indicates that the women decorated the litter in a special way to show their strong feelings for Solomon. (See:  Personification)
  * **and gaze on King Solomon**
 - "gaze" is to look at someone or something for a long time, usually with strong emotional feeling.
  * **bearing the crown**
 - "bearing" is to wear the crown.
