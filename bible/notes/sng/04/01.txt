===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your eyes are doves**  - Solomon uses the word "dove" as a way to express his love. (See: Metaphor)
  * **behind your veil **- "veil" is a piece of fine cloth covering the face of a woman, especially on the wedding day.
  * **Your hair is like a flock of goats**  - Goats are often dark in color and when they walk on the mountains they look like the long waves in a woman's hair. (See: Metaphor)
