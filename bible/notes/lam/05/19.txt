===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you reign forever, and your throne is from generation to generation.**- These two phrases mean generally the same thing. (See: Parallelism)
  *  **your throne** - God's throne represents his power and authority to rule as king. (See: Metonymy)
  *  **from generation to generation** - This means that God always rules, year after year. (See: Idiom)
  * **Restore our days as of old**  - "Restore our days" is a way to speak of restoring the people. AT: "Renew us so that we will be like we were long ago." (See: Metonymy)
  * **Why do you forget us forever? Will you forsake us for very, very long?**  - The author is complaining that God should remember his people and help them. (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **unless we truly are rejected and your anger towards us is too great!**  - Possible meanings are 1) that this is a cry of despair. AT - "It is really true that you will never stop being extremely angry with us!" (See UDB), or 2) that the author does not know the answer to the question. AT - "We don't know if you have truly rejected us and your anger towards us is too great!" (as the ULB). (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **unless we truly are rejected**  - AT: "unless you, God, have truly rejected us." (See: Active/Passive)
