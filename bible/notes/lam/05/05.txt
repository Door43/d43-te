===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They have pursued us**  - Possible meanings include 1) "It is as though those who pursue us have put their feet on our necks" (see UDB), or 2) "They pursue us very closely", or 3) "They put a yoke on our necks and pushed us to work as hard as animals" (See: Metaphor).
    * **They** - This refers to the invaders from Babylon
    * **We have reached out with our hands to the Egyptians**  - Reaching out their hands means they begged the Egyptians for help. (See: Metonymy)
    * **and the Assyrians to be filled with food**  - "And we begged the Assyrian people to give us food to eat." (See: Active/Passive)
    * **they are no more**  - This means that they have died. (See: Idiom)
    * **we bear their sins**  - This means that they receive the bad results of their sins.
