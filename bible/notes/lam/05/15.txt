===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the joy of our heart**  - This refers to joy that comes from deep within the person. (See: Synecdoche)
    * **The crown has fallen from our head!**  - Possible meanings are 1) "The wreaths of flowers have fallen off our heads", referring to flowers worn by young people in celebrations (See UDB) or 2) "the royal crown has fallen from our head" and we no longer rule as kings. (See: Metonymy)
