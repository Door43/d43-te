===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **there is no one to rescue us out of their hand**  - AT: "No one can rescue us from their power." (See: Metonymy)
  * **their**  - This refers to the Babylonian invaders.
  * **to get our bread**  - This refers to finding the cheapest and most basic food to eat. (See: Metonymy)
  * **in the face of the sword**  - AT: "despite the danger of robbers, who use swords as weapons." (See: Metonymy)
  * **Our skin is like an oven**  - Their skin is extremely hot because they are starving. (See: Simile)
  * **burnt up from the fever of famine** - Possible meanings are: 1) "we have a very high fever because we are extremely hungry" (see UDB) or 2) "our bodies are experiencing the result of being very hungry"
