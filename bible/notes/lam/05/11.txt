===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They have raped the women in Zion, the virgins in the cities of Judah.** - These two clauses mean generally the same thing. (See: Parallelism)
    * **they**  - This refers to the Babylonian invaders.
    * **They hung princes by their own hands**  - Possible meanings are 1) "Our enemies have hanged our leaders", using their own hands to do it (see UDB), or 2) "Our enemies have tied up the hands of the princes and suspended them by their hands."
