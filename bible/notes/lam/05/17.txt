===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Our hearts have become sick**  - This means that we feel deeply ill. (See: Synecdoche)
    * **our eyes have grown dim**  - This means that because of our deep sadness we cry and do not see well. (See: Synecdoche)
    * **jackals**  - These are small, wild, vicious dogs; their presence on Zion symbolizes that people are no longer living there.
