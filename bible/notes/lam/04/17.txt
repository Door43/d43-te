    * **our eyes** - This means "we". (See: Synecdoche)
    * **failed to find** - AT: "looked for but could not locate". (See: Litotes)
    * **They hunted our steps**  - AT: "They chased us."
    * **They** - "They" refers to the Babylonian invaders.
    * **our end was near...our end had come**  - Their "end" means their time to die.
