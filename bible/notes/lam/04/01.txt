===== Translation Notes: =====

A new poem begins. See [[te:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] â€‹and [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]].

  * **The gold has become completely tarnished; the purest gold has changed** - The people of Jerusalem are like gold that has lost its shine and value. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **holy stones** - This refers to the stones that made up the temple.
  * **at the head of every street** - AT: "at the beginning of every street" (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **sons of Zion** - This phrase refers to those who live in Jerusalem.
  * **they are regarded as nothing but clay jars made by potter's hands** - AT: "people consider them to be as worthless as the clay jars that potters make" (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
