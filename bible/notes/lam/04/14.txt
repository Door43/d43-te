===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They now wander as blind men in the streets**  - AT: "People wander aimlessly in the streets as if they could not see where they were going." (See: Simile)
  * **They now wander**  - Possible meanings of "they" are 1) the Jewish prophets and priests in Lam 2:13 (see UDB) or 2) the Jewish people in general.
  * **They are defiled by that blood**  - The prophets and priests are morally and ritually unclean because they are responsible for the people that the invaders killed. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **so none are able to even touch their clothes**  - In the Jewish Law, people were not allowed to touch unclean people, or they too would be made unclean.
