===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **iniquity **- The word used here can mean either 1) sin or 2) punishment for sin
  * **the daughter of my people**  - This is a Semitic way of referring to the people of Judah. (See: Idiom)
  * **the sin of Sodom**  - The author does not say what that sin was; on other passages the Bible says that they were oppressive and inhospitable, even to the point of trying to sexually attack visitors to their city.
  * **who was overthrown in a moment**  - AT: "which God destroyed in an instant of time." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **who**  - This refers to the city of Sodom.
  * **though no hands seized her**  - That is, God destroyed Sodom without the help of human soldiers. AT: "though no human soldiers captured Sodom." (See: Metonymy)
