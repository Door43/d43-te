===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **waste away**  - This refers to people losing body weight because of slow starvation. (See: Idiom)
  * **hunger for the crops of the field **- The people hungered for something that did not exist as the fields were empty.
  * **The hands of the compassionate women**  - AT: "Compassionate women". (See: Synecdoche)
  * **the daughter of my people**  - This is a Semitic expression that refers to the inhabitants of Jerusalem. (See: Idiom)
