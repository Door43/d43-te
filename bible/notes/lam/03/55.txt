===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I called upon your name** - AT: "I called to you for help" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **out of the lowest pit** - Possible meanings are 1) "from a very deep well" or 2) "from the grave."
    * **You heard my voice** - AT: "You heard what I said" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Do not hide your ear** - AT: "Do not refuse to listen" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **from my call for relief, from my cry for help** - These two phrases mean the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **you drew near** - To draw near means to come closer in order to help. AT: "You came to help me". (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
