===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **My eye runs down with streams of water** - Tears flow from his eyes like water flows in a river. (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **My eye flows out** - AT: "tears flow from my eyes" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **because of the crushing of the daughter of my people** - See how you translated this phrase in [[te:bible:notes:lam:02:11|2:11]].
    * **it will not cease...no end to it** - Here the word "it" refers to the tears flowing from his eyes. AT: "they will not cease...no end to them" (See: [[te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]])
