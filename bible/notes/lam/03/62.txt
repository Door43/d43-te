===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the lips of those that rose up against me** - AT: "the words that those who rose up against me spoke" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **those that rose up against me** - This refers to standing up to attack. AT: "those who assault me" (See: [[:te:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **their sitting or their rising up** - These two phrases taken together mean all the time. AT: "When they sit down and when they get up" or "Whenever they do anything" (See: [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]] and [[:te:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
